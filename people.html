Principal Investigator

Dr. Lin Li

Associate Professor, Department of Biomedical Engineering
College of Engineering, University of North Texas

Dr. Li directs the Li Lab's research in neuroengineering, epileptogenesis, electrophysiology, neuroimaging, brain network analysis, and photobiomodulation. His group develops multimodal approaches to detect epileptogenic brain networks and evaluate strategies for preventing or reducing neurological dysfunction.

He received his PhD in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Texas at Arlington in 2015 and his BS in Optoelectronic Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2009.
