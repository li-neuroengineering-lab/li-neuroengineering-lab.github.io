Detecting epileptogenesis before chronic epilepsy emerges.

The Li Lab develops multimodal biomarkers and neuromodulation strategies for epilepsy and related neurological disorders, combining electrophysiology, neuroimaging, network science, and photobiomodulation.

Research focus
